CLASSICAL


Frédéric Chopin



  • Nocturne Op. 9 No.2
  • Fantaisie-Impromptu
  • Etude Op. 10 No. 4

Chopin is one of the most famous composers in the Romantic era. He is famous for his expressive piano playing and the innovative works he composed for that instrument. Chopin is responsible for the development of modern piano technique and style. His talents were beyond emulation and had an impact on other musicians entirely out of proportion to the number of concerts he gave, only 30 public performances in 30 years of concertizing. Chopin's works for solo piano include about 61 mazurkas, 16 polonaises, 26 preludes, 27 études, 21 nocturnes, 20 waltzes, 3 sonatas, 4 ballades, 4 scherzos, 4 impromptus, and many individual pieces. I love listening to Classical music because it helps me keep calm and relax during my study periods.

ELECTRO SWING


Jamie Berry



  • Delight
  • Lost in the Rythm
  • Peeping Tom

Jamie Berry is English producer who has become renowned for his incorporation of American swing music into electronic dance music, helping to popularize the dance subgenre known as "electro-swing". He has performed at some of the U.K.’s biggest music festivals, including the Beat-Herder Festival. Jamie Berry is a well known Music Producer. His music makes people dance to the rythm. His songs have fantastic energy, mostly wanting people to move with the beat, paired with trumpets and saxophones at the spotlight, electro drum beats and bass drops at the sidelines, and with the classic piano as the background effect. All-in-all, Electro swing, or swing house, is an electronic dance music genre that combines the influence of vintage or modern swing and jazz mixed with house and hip hop.
