ENGLAND, LONDON

The image shows an riverview of what the city of London look like, featuring the large and famous clocktower, Big Ben

London is the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is a 21st-century city with history stretching back to Roman times. At its centre stand the imposing Houses of Parliament, the iconic ‘Big Ben’ clock tower and Westminster Abbey, site of British monarch coronations. Across the Thames River, the London Eye observation wheel provides panoramic views of the South Bank cultural complex, and the entire city. In order to travel to London England, one must have a passport and a VISA, then one must schedule a flight to the selected destination.
