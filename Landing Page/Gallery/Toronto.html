TORONTO, CANADA

The image shows an overview of what the city of Toronto look like, with colorful bright lights during the night

Toronto Canada is the capital of the province of Ontario, is a major Canadian city along Lake Ontario’s northwestern shore. It's a dynamic metropolis with a core of soaring skyscrapers, all dwarfed by the iconic, free-standing CN Tower. Toronto also has many green spaces, from the orderly oval of Queen’s Park to 400-acre High Park and its trails, sports facilities and zoo. In order to travel to Tokyo Japan, one must have a passport and a VISA, then one must schedule a flight to the selected destination.
